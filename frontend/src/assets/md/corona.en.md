When studying here:

- Always wear your mask, even when sitting down
- Don't keep extra spaces occupied
- Don't move chairs or tables, they have been placed with physical distancing rules in mind
- Be kind to the librarians, student volunteers and other students
- Feeling ill? stay home and study via the online discord library!

Good luck studying!

You can find the latest corona measures [here](https://www.ugent.be/en/news-events/coronameasures.htm)
